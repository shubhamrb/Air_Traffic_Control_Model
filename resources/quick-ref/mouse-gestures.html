Mouse gestures

Mouse hover

  • strip: show comment in tool tip
  • radar scope: show selection-to-mouse info
  • unpinned navpoints and elements on the radar scope: show short name, and frequency/channel if navaid
  • entry in navigator or ATC list: show a tool tip with true heading and distance from radar centre

Plain click

Left button:

  • on aircraft body, radar tag, strip or FPL: select
  • on radar background: deselect
  • on navpoint: fill navigator filter and instruction navpoint fields
  • on radio text chat message: recall dest./message in entry line
    • own message: recalls both
    • other: fill dest. and focus message field
  • with SHIFT on navpoint if selection contains a strip with a recognised route: add/remove enroute waypoint

Middle button:

  • on aircraft body, radar tag, strip or FPL: link to current selection if any and possible
  • with SHIFT on aircraft body, radar tag, strip or FPL (if selected and linked): unlink from rest of selection

Double-click

No SHIFT:

  • on strip: open and edit the strip detail sheet
  • on aircraft body or radar tag: edit linked strip if any
  • on rack, under strips: create new strip on rack
  • on loose strip bay background: create new strip in bay
  • on rack tab or column title: edit/delete rack
  • on radar navpoint: pin/unpin label
  • on navigator entry: indicate point on radar screen
  • on ATC: switch ATC chat panel to private channel
  • on notification: notification-dependant

With SHIFT:

  • on strip: indicate linked ACFT or XPDR identification
  • on ATC: indicate position if known
  • on aircraft body: remove hdg/alt./FL/speed assignments
  • on radar tag: reset text box position
  • on radar background: add custom text label (annotate radar)
  • on custom text label: remove label

Click & drag

Left button:

  • on radar scope if not locked: pan view
  • aircraft body: vector/instruct (press right button while dragging to abort; solo: instructs callsign; teacher: direct control)
    • without SHIFT: assign heading or instruct taxi
    • with SHIFT, dragging vertically: assign alt./FL
    • with SHIFT, dragging horizontally: assign speed
  • radar tag: move tag
  • strip:
    • within/between racks or loose bays: rerack/reorder/reposition flight strip
    • with SHIFT on loose strip: move and keep within bay, pushing edges as needed
    • drop on handover list: hand linked aircraft over to ATC
    • drop on shelf: shelve strip, e.g. when releasing a contact

Right button:

  • on radar scope without SHIFT: quick heading and distance measuring tool + show ground elevation if available
  • on radar scope with SHIFT as teacher: create new traffic
  • in tower view window when not following an aircraft (FlightGear input): look around
